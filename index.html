A Legacy of Light

This academy exists because of one man's love for his father — and his father's love for the Quran. Every student who joins carries that legacy forward.

إِذَا مَاتَ الْإِنْسَانُ انْقَطَعَ عَنْهُ عَمَلُهُ إِلَّا مِنْ ثَلَاثَةٍ "When a person dies, all their deeds end except three: a continuous charity, beneficial knowledge, or a righteous child who prays for them." Sahih Muslim · 1631
